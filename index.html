How it works

Three clicks from press play
to the full breakdown.

01

Open any finance video

Graham Stephan, Meet Kevin, CNBC — whatever you already watch. BullNotes wakes up the moment it spots a finance video.

02

Hit the BullNotes button

A floating button sits in the bottom-right corner. One click pulls the full transcript and runs the analysis.

BullNotes ▸
03

Read the signal, skip the noise

Every ticker, a plain-English read on the video's sentiment, the creator's take and main risk per stock — plus the one stock they're most excited about.

The panel

A whole video,
distilled into one panel.

Overall market sentiment

A plain-English read on the video's thesis and how bullish it skews. Collapse it when you just want the tickers.

BUY / HOLD / SELL per ticker

Every stock gets its own card with the company, a clear signal, the creator's take, and the main risk.

Live prices & mini charts

Real-time price, daily move and after-hours quotes on every ticker, plus a sparkline that turns green or red to match the day.

Top Pick & jump-to timestamps

Flags the one stock the creator pushes hardest. Timestamp chips jump the video straight to where each stock is discussed.

Also-mentioned, kept separate

Stocks only named in passing are listed on their own, so the real deep-dives stay front and center.

Honest by design

Flags tickers it only inferred, warns you when a video is stale, and never dresses up automated signals as financial advice.
